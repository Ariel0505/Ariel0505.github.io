Volleyball

Volleyball is a sport that any person can play whether your a girl or boy. When playing volleyball you can use any part of your body however you are not supposed to hold the ball.
You cannot only hold the ball when you are serving or handling the ball to the other team. When handing the other team a ball you have to roll it under the net. There is a net between you and
the other team amd you and the other team cannot touch or cross the net. There are 3 sets per game. however when it is finals or a very important game it goes to 5 sets. For the 3 set game you
have to win 2 sets. To win a set you have to have a total of 25 points. However for a 5 set game the fifth set will be up to 15 points. If it goes to 14 14 or 24 24 for the points there will
be a tiebreaker whoever has the lead of two points.
